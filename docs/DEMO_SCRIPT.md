# Project M8: The Mystical Walkthrough (Demo Script)

This script provides a step-by-step narrative for demonstrating the M8 Phase 1 ecosystem to stakeholders.

## 🏁 Preparation
1.  **Open URL**: Start at the **Author Dashboard** (`/`).
2.  **Login**: Use the Expert Persona `john@m8.com`.
3.  **Sandbox Reset**: 
    *   Click the **Blue Toolbox** (Bottom Right).
    *   Tap **"Reset Environment"** to purge all old mystical data and re-seed with a clean slate.

---

## 🎭 Act 1: The Divine Creation (Author Experience)
**Narrative**: *"Meet John, a creator who wants to send a mystical guidance set to his friend Bob."*

1.  **Dashboard**: Show the "Expert Dashboard" with existing sets and history.
2.  **The Drafting Flow**:
    *   Click **"Create New Set"**.
    *   Enter a few answers (e.g., *"The stars align"*, *"My crystal ball says NO"*).
    *   Point out the **70-character constraint** (M8 Philosophy: Half-tweet length).
3.  **The Simulator (Zero UI)**:
    *   Toggle between **Phone** and **Watch** profiles.
    *   Show how the text floats in the mystical blue pool.
    *   Briefly toggle **"Pure Mode"** to show the distraction-free creator view.

---

## 💸 Act 2: The Mystical Transaction (Checkout)
**Narrative**: *"John is ready to send his set to Bob. He pays a mock fee of $2 for the distribution."*

1.  **Targeting**: Enter Bob's email `bob@m8.com`.
2.  **Mock Checkout**:
    *   Click **"Complete Checkout & Dispatch"**.
    *   Interact with the **MockPaymentModal** (Glassmorphic Stripe Simulation).
    *   Click **"Pay $2.00 (MOCK)"**.
3.  **The Interceptor**:
    *   Point to the **Toolbox Drawer**.
    *   Show the **"Intercepted Connectivity"** log.
    *   *"Look—the SMS/Email deep link was just generated and 'sent' to Bob's device instantly."*

---

## 🛡️ Act 3: The Guardian Gateway (Parental Review)
**Narrative**: *"But what if Bob was under 13? In M8, safety is built-in. Let's see how a Guardian intervenes."*

1.  **Setup**: Navigate to `/guardian/approve?id=DEMO_GIFT_ID`. 
2.  **The Portal**: Show the zero-login, deep-linked **Guardian Review Portal**.
3.  **Decision**:
    *   Show off the **Reject** logic (provide a reason like *"Too spooky for Timmy"*).
    *   Then, perform an **Approve** action to activate the mystical link.
    *   *"The Guardian has blessed the content. It is now ready for Bob's device."*

---

## 🌀 Act 4: Acceptance & The Orb (Questioner Activity)
**Narrative**: *"Finally, Bob receives the link on his mobile device or watch."*

1.  **Flutter Acceptance**:
    *   Show the **Notification View** overlaying the Orb.
    *   Demonstrate **Light Shake to Accept** vs **Violent Shake to Reject**.
2.  **The Interaction**:
    *   Perform a **Light Shake** to load the set.
    *   Perform a final **Question + Shake** to reveal John's custom answer floating to the surface.

---

## 🧹 Post-Demo Cleanup
*   Return to the **Toolbox** and click **"Reset Environment"** for the next presentation.
